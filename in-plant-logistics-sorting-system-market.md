<p><p>In-plant logistics sorting systems are essential for efficient material handling and distribution within facilities. The horizontal cross band sorting system operates by moving items horizontally across a conveyor band, optimizing space and speed for sorting processes. In contrast, the vertical cross band sorting system utilizes vertical movement, enabling the sorting of items over multiple levels, which maximizes vertical space and streamlines workflow. Both systems enhance operational efficiency and accuracy in sorting while adapting to different facility designs and requirements.</p></p>
