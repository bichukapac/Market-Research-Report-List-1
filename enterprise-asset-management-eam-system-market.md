<p><p>Enterprise Asset Management (EAM) systems can be deployed in two primary market types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based EAM solutions are hosted on remote servers and accessed via the internet, offering scalability, flexibility, and reduced IT maintenance costs. Conversely, on-premises EAM systems are installed locally on a company’s servers, providing more control over data security and customization but requiring higher upfront investments and ongoing maintenance. Each type serves distinct organizational needs based on resource availability and operational preferences.</p></p>
