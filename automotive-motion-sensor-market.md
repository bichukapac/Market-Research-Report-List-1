<p><ul><li>Accelerometer</li><li>Gyroscope</li><li>Combo Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive motion sensor market comprises three main types: accelerometers, gyroscopes, and combo sensors. Accelerometers measure linear acceleration, providing data on vehicle speed and direction changes. Gyroscopes detect rotational motion, aiding in stability and orientation assessments. Combo sensors integrate both functions, enhancing accuracy and efficiency in applications like vehicle dynamics control and navigation systems. These sensors play a crucial role in advanced driver-assistance systems (ADAS), enabling features such as electronic stability control and autonomous driving technologies.</p></p>
