<p><p>Hydrofiber dressings are advanced wound care products designed to manage exudate effectively while providing a moist healing environment. Composed primarily of sodium carboxymethyl cellulose, these dressings absorb excess fluid and convert it into a gel, promoting faster healing and reducing the risk of infection. They are particularly beneficial for chronic wounds, such as diabetic ulcers and pressure sores, due to their ability to maintain optimal moisture levels.</p><p>The Hydrofiber Dressings Market is experiencing significant growth, driven by an increasing prevalence of chronic wounds, a rising aging population, and heightened awareness regarding advanced wound care. Hospitals and healthcare facilities are increasingly adopting these dressings due to their effectiveness and ease of use. The market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 14.6% during the forecast period, fueled by innovations in dressing materials and formulations that enhance absorption and comfort. Additionally, the expansion of healthcare infrastructure in emerging markets and the growing trend toward home healthcare services are further propelling market growth.
