<p><ul><li>In-ear Earbud</li><li>Bone Conduction</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The swimming headphone market primarily features two types: in-ear earbuds and bone conduction headphones. In-ear earbuds are designed to fit snugly inside the ear, providing excellent sound isolation and quality while being waterproof to withstand underwater use. Bone conduction headphones, on the other hand, transmit sound through the bones of the skull, allowing users to hear ambient sounds while still enjoying music. Both types cater to swimmers seeking a seamless audio experience during workouts or leisure time in the water.</p></p>
