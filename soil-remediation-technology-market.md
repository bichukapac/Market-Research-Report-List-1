<p><p>Soil remediation technology encompasses three primary market types: biological, physical, and chemical remediation. Biological remediation leverages microorganisms to degrade contaminants naturally and is eco-friendly. Physical remediation involves the removal or containment of pollutants using methods like excavation or thermal treatment. Chemical remediation employs chemical agents to neutralize or transform harmful substances in the soil. Each method offers distinct advantages and can be selected based on the specific contaminants, site conditions, and regulatory requirements.</p></p>
