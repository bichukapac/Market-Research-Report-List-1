<p><ul><li>Singlemode SFP Module</li><li>Multimode SFP Module</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The SFP module market is categorized mainly into two types: singlemode and multimode SFP modules. Singlemode SFP modules are designed for long-distance transmission, utilizing a narrow fiber core that allows for greater distances, typically over 10 kilometers. In contrast, multimode SFP modules support shorter distances, using a wider core to enable multiple light propagation paths. Each type caters to specific networking needs, influencing factors like bandwidth, distance, and application suitability in various telecommunication and data center environments.</p></p>
