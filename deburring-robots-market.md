<p><p>The deburring robots market is divided into two main types: deburring on CNC machines and deburring performed by standalone robots. Deburring on CNC involves integrating automated deburring functions within computer numerical control systems, enhancing efficiency and precision. In contrast, standalone deburring robots operate independently, effectively removing rough edges and burrs from various components. Both types improve product quality, reduce manual labor, and streamline manufacturing processes, catering to industries such as automotive, aerospace, and metal fabrication.</p></p>
