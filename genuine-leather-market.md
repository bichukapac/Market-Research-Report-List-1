<p><p>The genuine leather market consists primarily of two types: top-grain leather and split leather. Top-grain leather is derived from the upper layer of animal hides, noted for its durability and natural grain finish, offering a premium quality look and feel. In contrast, split leather is made from the lower layers and usually has a suede-like texture, featuring less durability and strength compared to top-grain. Both types cater to different consumer needs, from luxury products to more affordable options.</p></p>
