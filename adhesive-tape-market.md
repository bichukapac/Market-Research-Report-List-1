<p><p>The adhesive tape market can be categorized into three main types: PP backed, paper backed, and PVC backed tapes. PP backed tapes are known for their moisture resistance and durability, making them suitable for packaging and insulation applications. Paper backed tapes are eco-friendly and typically used in arts, crafts, and general purpose tasks due to their easy tear and versatility. PVC backed tapes offer excellent electrical insulation and flexibility, commonly used in electrical industries and heavy-duty applications. Each type serves distinct functions based on material properties.</p></p>
