<p><strong>The Gas Valves Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gate Valves</li><li>Control Valves</li><li>Ball Valves</li><li>Butterfly Valve</li><li>Plug Valves</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The gas valves market comprises several key types, each serving distinct purposes. Gate valves allow or restrict flow, primarily for on/off control. Control valves regulate flow and pressure, facilitating precise process management. Ball valves enable quick shutoff with low resistance and high flow rates, while butterfly valves provide efficient flow control in compact designs. Plug valves offer reliable sealing and easy operation for throttling and on/off functions. Together, these valves cater to diverse applications across industries such as oil and gas, manufacturing, and utilities.</p></p>
