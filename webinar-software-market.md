<p><p>The webinar software market consists of two main types: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. Cloud-based webinar software is hosted on remote servers, allowing users to access the platform via the internet. This type offers scalability, ease of use, and automatic updates. On-premises software, on the other hand, is installed locally on a company’s servers, providing greater control over data security and customization. Both types cater to diverse organizational needs, with cloud solutions often favored for flexibility and on-premises for security and compliance.</p></p>
