<p><p>Common mode filters with ESD protection are essential for mitigating electromagnetic interference while safeguarding sensitive electronic components from electrostatic discharge. The market is divided into bidirectional and unidirectional types. Bidirectional filters can handle voltage surges in both directions, making them suitable for AC applications and differential signals. In contrast, unidirectional filters only protect against surges in one direction, typically used in DC applications. Both types enhance device reliability in diverse electronic systems, from consumer electronics to industrial equipment.</p></p>
