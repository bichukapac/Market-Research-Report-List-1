<p><p>The lamination film market is segmented into several types, each catering to specific applications. BOPP (Biaxially Oriented Polypropylene) based lamination films are known for their clarity and moisture resistance, making them ideal for packaging. BOPET (Biaxially Oriented Polyethylene Terephthalate) based films offer superior strength and thermal stability, suitable for high-end applications. BOPA (Biaxially Oriented Polyamide) films provide excellent barrier properties, particularly in food packaging. Additionally, the "Others" category includes films made from various polymers for niche applications, enhancing versatility in the market.</p></p>
