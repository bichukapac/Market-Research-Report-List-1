<p><p>Bale spear market types are categorized into double-fork and multi-fork designs based on their functionality and application. The double-fork bale spear features two prongs, providing stability and ease in handling standard round or square bales. In contrast, multi-fork bale spears, equipped with three or more prongs, offer enhanced grip and support for larger or heavier loads. Both types are essential in agricultural operations, facilitating efficient transportation and storage of hay and straw bales while minimizing damage.</p></p>
