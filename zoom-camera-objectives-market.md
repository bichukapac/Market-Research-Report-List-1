<p><ul><li>Optical zoom</li><li>Extended optical zoom</li><li>Digital zoom</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The zoom camera objectives market features three main types: optical zoom, extended optical zoom, and digital zoom. Optical zoom uses physical lenses to magnify images, maintaining image quality even at high levels. Extended optical zoom combines optical and digital zoom techniques to enhance range while preserving clarity. Digital zoom, on the other hand, enlarges a portion of the image digitally, often resulting in reduced quality. Each type caters to different consumer needs, balancing convenience and image fidelity.</p></p>
