<p><p>Lidar systems for the wind market are crucial for optimizing wind energy generation. Ground-mounted Lidar systems are deployed at various distances from wind turbine arrays, measuring wind speeds and directions to improve site assessment and turbine placement. Nacelle-mounted Lidar systems are installed directly on the turbine nacelle, providing real-time wind data in the rotor's operational environment, enhancing performance monitoring and predictive maintenance. Together, these technologies help maximize efficiency, reduce downtime, and improve energy output in wind farms.</p></p>
