<p><p>The vehicle refrigeration equipment market is divided into two main types: Single Temperature Type and Multi-Temperature Type. Single Temperature Type systems maintain a uniform temperature throughout the cargo area, ideal for transporting specific goods like frozen foods or pharmaceuticals. In contrast, Multi-Temperature Type systems feature multiple compartments with varying temperature settings, enabling the simultaneous transport of diverse products, such as fresh produce and frozen items. This flexibility caters to the needs of varied supply chains and enhances operational efficiency.</p></p>
