<p><p>The insulation mica tape market comprises mainly two types: mica glass tape and mica polyester tape. Mica glass tape is made by combining mica with glass fibers, offering excellent thermal stability and electrical insulation, making it suitable for high-temperature applications. In contrast, mica polyester tape utilizes a polyester backing, providing flexibility and lightweight properties while still maintaining good insulation capabilities. Both types are widely used in electrical and thermal insulation in industries such as manufacturing, aerospace, and automotive.</p></p>
