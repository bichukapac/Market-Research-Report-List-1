<p><strong>The Stock Music Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Royalty free (RF)</li><li>Rights managed (RM)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The stock music market consists primarily of two types: Royalty Free (RF) and Rights Managed (RM). RF music allows buyers to pay a one-time fee for unlimited use across various projects without ongoing royalties. This makes it budget-friendly and versatile. In contrast, RM music involves licensing fees based on factors like usage, duration, and audience size, requiring additional payments for specific rights. This can provide exclusive access to high-quality tracks but may be more complex and costly.</p></p>
