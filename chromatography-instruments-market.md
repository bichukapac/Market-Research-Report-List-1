<p><p>The chromatography instruments market is categorized into several types, each serving distinct analytical needs. Gas chromatography instruments utilize gas as the mobile phase to separate volatile compounds. Liquid chromatography employs liquid solvents for the analysis of various samples, including pharmaceuticals. Supercritical fluid chromatography combines gas and liquid properties for enhanced resolution. Thin-layer chromatography involves a stationary layer on a plate for separating mixtures. Other methods may include ion chromatography and affinity chromatography, catering to specialized applications in research and industry.</p></p>
