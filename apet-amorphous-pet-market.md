<p><p>Amorphous PET (APET) is a versatile thermoplastic used primarily in packaging and manufacturing applications. The APET market is segmented into chips and granules. Chips are small, pre-formed pieces ideal for processes like injection molding and thermoforming, offering excellent clarity and mechanical properties. Granules, on the other hand, are larger particles often used in various extrusion applications. Both forms are esteemed for their recyclability, durability, and resistance to chemicals, making them popular choices in multiple industries, particularly in food packaging.</p></p>
