<p><p>The cell phone signal booster market is primarily divided into two types: analog signal boosters and smart signal boosters. Analog signal boosters amplify existing cellular signals using basic technology for improved reception. They are typically simpler to install and suitable for small areas. In contrast, smart signal boosters utilize advanced technology to optimize and manage signal reception and distribution. They can adapt to varying signal conditions, often providing better performance in complex environments, making them ideal for larger spaces or urban settings.</p></p>
