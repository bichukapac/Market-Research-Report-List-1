<p><p>The Polyester Drawn Yarn market is primarily divided into two types: Roving and Spun Yarn. Roving refers to a loosely twisted strand of polyester fibers, providing a base for further processing into various textile products. Spun Yarn, on the other hand, involves twisting fibers together to create a more durable and textured yarn, suitable for weaving and knitting. Both types cater to diverse applications in the textile industry, with Roving often used for non-woven fabrics and Spun Yarn for traditional fabric production.</p></p>
