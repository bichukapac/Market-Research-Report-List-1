<p><p>The bone scissors market comprises three primary types: double joint bone scissors, single joint bone scissors, and rib scissors. Double joint bone scissors are designed for cutting tough bone with greater maneuverability and precision, featuring two moving joints. Single joint bone scissors focus on stability and strength, suitable for straightforward cuts in orthopedic procedures. Rib scissors, specifically crafted for delicate rib dissection, enable precise cuts while minimizing tissue damage. Together, these variations cater to diverse surgical needs in orthopedic and trauma surgeries.</p></p>
