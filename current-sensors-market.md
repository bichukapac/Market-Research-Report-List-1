<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Current Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hall-Effect Current Sensors</li><li>Shunt Current Sensors</li><li>TMR Current Sensors</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The current sensors market includes various types that cater to diverse applications. Hall-effect current sensors utilize magnetic fields to measure current, offering high accuracy and isolation. Shunt current sensors work by measuring the voltage drop across a resistive element, providing a cost-effective solution for current monitoring. TMR (Tunneling Magnetoresistance) current sensors leverage magnetoresistive effects for higher sensitivity and efficiency. Other types may include current transformers and Rogowski coils, addressing specialized needs in industrial and automotive sectors.</p></p>
