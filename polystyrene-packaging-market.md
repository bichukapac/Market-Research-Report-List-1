<p><p>The polystyrene packaging market is primarily divided into two types: low-density and high-density polystyrene. Low-density polystyrene (LDPS) is lightweight and offers excellent insulation, making it ideal for products like food containers and protective packaging. In contrast, high-density polystyrene (HDPS) is more rigid and durable, suitable for applications requiring enhanced strength, such as electronics packaging and construction materials. Both types serve distinct market needs, contributing to the versatility and widespread use of polystyrene in various packaging solutions.</p></p>
