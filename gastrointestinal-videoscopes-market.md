<p><p>The gastrointestinal videoscope market is divided into two main types: hard mirror and flexible soft mirror. Hard mirror videoscopes feature rigid structures, allowing for precise imaging in certain diagnostic procedures but are limited in maneuverability. In contrast, flexible soft mirror videoscopes offer enhanced adaptability and are designed to navigate complex anatomical structures, making them more widely used in gastroenterology. The choice between these types depends on the specific medical requirements and the degree of patient comfort needed during examinations.</p></p>
