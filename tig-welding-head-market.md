<p><p>The TIG welding head market encompasses various welding techniques, including butt welding and spot seam welding. Butt welding involves joining two pieces of metal end-to-end, providing a strong, continuous bond ideal for structural applications. In contrast, spot seam welding is a localized method that joins overlapping metal sheets using heat generated by electrical resistance, creating strong welds in a compact area. Both methods are essential in manufacturing and construction, offering distinct advantages in durability and precision.</p></p>
